Capital

We will take out a small business loan to fund our business. We are relying on our good credit scores to give us credibility.









Location

We’ll be based in Los Angeles. For our small business retail will be done mainly online however we will have to start with one in-store location in LA. Once the business starts to generate revenue we’ll start expanding our locations to include more in person stores. Aside from in person stores, some other spaces required would be a warehouse for storing jewelry, space to make and produce the jewelry, as well as a design/office space to work on product design and marketing.









Personnel

For our jewelry business we’ll need a couple different types of employees. We will need to have high level employees who work with product design and figuring out how each piece of jewelry should be made. We will also need a high level marketing team to be able to market our jewelry designs. Some of the lower level employees needed will be workers to manufacture the designs, and in person store employees to be cashiers, and run the storefronts. Our high level employees will need to be full time however some of the lower level employees like store cashiers can be part time.









Equipment

The basic equipment that will be needed for our jewellery business would be several different kinds of pliers, wire cutters, a flush cutter, a ruler, disk punches and a jeweler’s saw.









Inventory

To keep our business in supply, we’re going to need beads, gems, wire, chains, charms, clasps, jump rings, ear wires and extender chains. All of these things are absolutely necessary to make jewelry!





Profit and Loss Projections

We will not be making a profit initially because it will take time for people to discover our company. Once we have a few customers, they will hopefully spread the word about our jewelry and this will increase our number of sales. We will have social media pages and other methods of advertising that increase sales. Once our pages kick off, there will be a large profit increase.
